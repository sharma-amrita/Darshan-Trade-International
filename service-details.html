Services Provided by Darshan Trade International Pvt. Ltd.

At Darshan Trade International Pvt. Ltd., we deliver a comprehensive suite of hardware services tailored to construction and home improvement projects. Our expert consultation helps clients select the ideal materials, whether it’s G.I pipes, MS channels, beams, or TMT bars, ensuring that every project is equipped with the best solutions. We offer bulk order fulfillment and nationwide delivery, guaranteeing timely and secure transport to any location in Nepal. Our extensive selection features top brands across all product categories, allowing customers to choose the best options for their needs.


In addition to a broad product range, we excel at sourcing hard-to-find materials for special projects. We provide essential welding and fabrication supplies, including welding rods and steel flat bars, alongside free loading assistance and flexible payment options for all customers. Contractors benefit from dedicated account management, simplifying large order processing and billing. To ensure quality, we offer on-site delivery inspections for bulk purchases, and our hassle-free return and exchange policy ensures any discrepancies are quickly resolved. We also support eco-conscious construction with a range of sustainable materials. Our 24/7 customer support hotline ensures that help is always just a call away for inquiries, order tracking, or assistance with technical issues.

  • Offering sustainable, environmentally conscious materials.
  • Hassle-free exchanges for damaged or incorrect materials.
  • Round-the-clock hotline for inquiries, tracking, and technical assistance.
